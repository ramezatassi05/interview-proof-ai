# InterviewProof.ai — Brand Context for Carousel Scripts

## What InterviewProof Is

InterviewProof is a job-specific interview diagnostic that tells you exactly what will get you rejected — before the interview happens. Upload your resume and job description, get a readiness score, rejection risks, predicted questions, AI coaching, and a prioritized fix plan in 60 seconds.

**One-liner:** "Know Exactly What Will Sink You"
**Brand badge:** Interview Intelligence Platform
**Price:** Free preview → $9-23 credit bundles (5 credits = 1 full diagnostic)

## Target Audience

**Primary:** College students and new grads (18-26) — interns and entry-level candidates preparing for competitive interviews in tech, finance, and consulting.

**Psychographic profile:**
- First-time interview anxiety is real — they don't know what they don't know
- Imposter syndrome is the default
- TikTok IS their search engine (they search TikTok before Google)
- Price-sensitive but will pay $9 for certainty (two boba teas)
- Peer-influenced — if a friend says "this helped me get Google," they try it immediately
- Information-hungry — consume prep content obsessively
- Skeptical of generic AI advice — they've tried ChatGPT and gotten useless results
- Time-pressured — interview is in days, not months

## Content Pillars for Carousels

### Pillar 1: "Things Recruiters Will Never Tell You"
- Insider knowledge format — feels like a friend in recruiting
- Examples: "3 things on your resume that make recruiters say 'next' in 7 seconds"

### Pillar 2: "I Tested My Resume Against [Company]"
- Screen recording / reaction format — before/after scores
- Examples: "I tested my resume against Google's SWE intern role and got a 43/100"

### Pillar 3: "Interview Prep Secrets / Hacks"
- Quick tips, actionable advice
- Examples: "35% of your interview score comes from ONE thing (and it's not LeetCode)"

### Pillar 4: "Day-in-the-Life / Interview Journey"
- Authentic prep journey content
- Examples: "I have 7 days until my Amazon interview. Here's my AI-generated study plan."

### Pillar 5: "Shocking Statistics"
- Industry facts with dramatic reveals
- Examples: "250 people applied for your dream job. Here's why only 4 got interviews."

### Pillar 6: "Referral / Community"
- Engagement bait, challenges, comparisons
- Examples: "My roommate and I compared our interview scores. The results were BRUTAL."

## Hook Formula (InterviewProof-Specific)

Every carousel hook should follow this emotional arc:
1. **Create "oh no, that's me"** (fear/recognition)
2. **Reveal the hidden problem** (education)
3. **Show the fix exists** (resolution)
4. **The fix is fast and specific** (empowerment — 60 seconds, $9, not $200)

## CTA Templates (Soft, Organic — Never Hard Sell)

Use these on the final slide. Rotate between them:
- "Link in bio — 60 seconds to know your rejection risks"
- "Free preview, no credit card. Just upload and see."
- "I put the link in my bio if you want to try it"
- "This saved me hours of wasted prep. Link in bio."
- "Find out your score before your next interview → link in bio"
- "Follow for more interview intel that actually works"
- "Save this for when interview season hits"

**IMPORTANT:** The product should appear naturally — "the tool I used" or "this thing I found." Never a hard sell. Lead with education, reveal product organically.

- vs LeetCode: "Grinding without knowing your gaps is studying for the wrong exam."

## Tone & Voice

- Confident but not arrogant
- Evidence-based, blunt, direct
- Feels like a smart friend giving you real talk
- Uses "you" language — always speaking to the viewer
- Slightly urgent — "your interview is coming, are you ready?"
- Never preachy or lecture-y
- Memes, relatability, and shared anxiety are welcome
