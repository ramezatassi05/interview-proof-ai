# 2Ô∏è‚É£ DIAGNOSTIC REPORT ENHANCEMENTS

## 2.1 Interview Archetype Profile

Classify student as:

- Technical Potential, Low Polish
- Strong Theoretical, Weak Execution
- Resume-Strong, System-Weak
- Balanced but Unproven
- High Ceiling, Low Volume Practice

Why:
Students love identity signals. It increases memorability and shareability.

---

## 2.2 Interview Round Forecast

Break down pass probability by round:

- Online Assessment: 72%
- Technical Round 1: 61%
- System Design: 38%
- Behavioral: 54%

This makes the product feel predictive.

---

## 2.3 Cognitive Risk Map (Spider Chart)

Dimensions:
- Algorithm Speed
- Concept Recall
- Communication Clarity
- Depth vs Breadth
- Resume Signal Strength

Purpose:
Visual complexity = perceived depth.

---

## 2.4 Career Trajectory Projection

Add forecasting layer:

"If no additional prep: pass probability declines due to competition velocity."

"If study plan completed in 6 weeks: projected improvement +18%."

This moves from scoring ‚Üí modeling.

---

## 2.5 Recruiter Red Flag Simulation

Rename "Risks" to:
> Recruiter Red Flags

Each includes:
- Likelihood recruiter notices
- Severity impact
- Suggested fix
- Resume rewrite suggestion
